What is MacroLogix TMS?

One platform. Every mile covered.

MacroLogix TMS is a browser-based transportation management system built specifically for owner-operators and small-to-mid fleet carriers. No software to install, no IT team required.

🗺️
Dispatch & Live Map
Assign loads, track drivers, and manage your entire fleet from a single dispatch board with a live canvas map.
📱
Driver Mobile App
Drivers log arrivals, departures, and uploads from their phone — no app store download needed.
🏢
Customer Portal
Give your customers real-time shipment visibility, invoice access, BOL/POD downloads, and a load request form.

Everything your operation needs
